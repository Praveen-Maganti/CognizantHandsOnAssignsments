Time Complexity
Bubble Sort: O(n²) in most cases because it compares and swaps many elements.
Quick Sort: O(n log n) on average, making it much faster for large datasets.
Why Quick Sort is Better

Quick Sort sorts data much faster than Bubble Sort when there are many customer orders. It divides the data into smaller parts and sorts them efficiently.

Suitable for Customer Orders

For a large number of customer orders, Quick Sort is the better choice because it provides faster sorting and better performance than Bubble Sort.
